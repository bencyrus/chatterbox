# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and delete information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

When you use the app, we collect:

* **Audio recordings** that you choose to record in the app
* **Account information**, such as your email address, used for authentication
* **An internal account ID** used to associate your recordings with your account
* **Usage information**, such as how users interact with speaking subjects and features, used to improve the app experience

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* Store and manage your speaking recordings
* Provide feedback and speaking-related features
* Understand how users interact with the app and improve the user experience
* Operate and maintain the core functionality of the app

Audio recordings and related data are uploaded to and stored on servers hosted on Google Cloud Platform.

## Analytics

We collect limited analytics data to better understand how the app is used and to improve features and content.
This data is used only to improve the app and is **not used for advertising or tracking across other apps or services**.

## Data Retention and Deletion

We retain user data only while an account is active.

To request account deletion and removal of associated data, users may contact us by email at:
**[realbencyrus@gmail.com](mailto:realbencyrus@gmail.com)**

Upon account deletion, audio recordings and related account data are permanently removed from our systems.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell user data.

We do not share personal data with advertisers or data brokers.
Data may be shared only with service providers required to operate the app (such as cloud hosting and analytics services).

## Tracking and Advertising

We do not use personal data, including audio recordings or email addresses, for tracking or targeted advertising purposes.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
